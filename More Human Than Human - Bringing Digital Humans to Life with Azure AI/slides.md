- 🎓 **Education:** virtual teachers and trainers
- 💬 **Support:** consistent, multilingual avatars
- 📹 **Marketing:** scalable content without cameras
- ♿ **Accessibility:** inclusive communication and engagement

# Building Responsibly

1. Always disclose synthetic media
2. Use **consented** or **custom-built avatars**
3. Avoid identity impersonation
4. Respect Azure AI’s **Responsible AI Standard**

> “Technology is neutral. It’s our intentions that shape its ethics.”
